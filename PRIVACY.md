## Data Collected

YouTube Detox collects the following data locally on your device:

- YouTube video watch time and session durations
- Page navigation events on youtube.com
- Daily usage statistics
- User-configured settings and goals

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's `chrome.storage` API.

If you enable optional backend sync, data is transmitted to a self-hosted server that you configure. No data is sent to any third-party service.

## Data Sharing

YouTube Detox does **not** sell, share, or transmit your data to any third parties.

## Authentication

Google Sign-In is used solely to identify your account for optional backend sync. No data from your Google account is accessed beyond your basic profile (name, email).
